WHAT IS AUGUMENTED REALITY ?

Augmented reality, commonly abbreviated "AR",is
computer-generated content overlaid on a real world environment.One of the best example is Pokémon Go which is a popular video game that uses augmented reality.uses your smartphone's GPS signal to detect your location. As you walk around, your avatar is overlaid on a real-world map, you can attempt to catch a Pokémon,it shows up with a real-word background created by your smartphone's camera.
